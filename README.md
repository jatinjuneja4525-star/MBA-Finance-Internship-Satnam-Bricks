## Overview
This project was completed as part of my 3-month internship at **Satnam Bricks Industry, Darbhanga**, where I worked as a **Marketing Executive Intern**. The study focuses on market analysis, costing, pricing dynamics, and financial viability of **fly ash bricks** as a sustainable alternative to traditional red bricks.

---

## Role & Duration
**Role:** Marketing Executive Intern  
**Duration:** 3 Months  

---

## Objectives
- To analyze market demand and customer awareness for fly ash bricks  
- To study pricing and costing structures in the brick manufacturing industry  
- To evaluate the financial and sustainability benefits of fly ash bricks compared to red bricks  
- To understand how cost optimization and value proposition influence managerial decisions  

---

## Key Responsibilities & Work Performed
- Conducted **field surveys** to assess customer preferences, awareness, and adoption trends  
- Prepared a **market research paper** analyzing demand conditions and competitive landscape  
- Studied **costing components**, pricing strategies, and operational cost differences between fly ash bricks and clay bricks  
- Analyzed the **financial feasibility** and long-term benefits of fly ash bricks from a business and environmental perspective  
- Assisted in linking market insights with managerial and strategic decision-making  

---

## Tools & Methodology
- Primary data collection through surveys and interviews  
- Secondary research using industry reports and government regulations  
- Qualitative and quantitative analysis of market and costing data  

---

## Key Learnings
- Practical exposure to **cost analysis, pricing strategy, and demand forecasting**  
- Understanding of **sustainable business models** and their financial implications  
- Improved analytical thinking, market research skills, and business communication  
- Hands-on experience in applying theoretical concepts to real-world business scenarios  

---

## Outcome & MBA Finance Alignment
This project played a significant role in shaping my interest in pursuing an **MBA in Finance**. By working on market analysis, costing structures, and financial evaluation of sustainable products, I gained clarity on how financial insights drive strategic business decisions. The experience strengthened my aspiration to build advanced skills in financial analysis, managerial finance, and strategic planning.
